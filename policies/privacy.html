Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2025

At LilRoby Revive, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with LilRoby Revive:

  • Email Address: This is collected during registration or account creation.
  • Username: Your chosen username for the platform.
  • User ID: Assigned to you upon account creation.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Account management and authentication.
  • Communication with you, including updates on the service or any issues related to your account.
  • User support and troubleshooting.

3. Who Has Access to Your Information

Admins: Our site admins have access to your email, username, and user ID for site administration purposes, including troubleshooting and user support.

Moderators: Moderators do not have access to your personal data.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and will take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. No Cookies

Currently, we do not use cookies on LilRoby Revive. However, if this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy to reflect that.

6. Deleting Your Information

If you would like to delete your account and all associated personal information, please send an email to info@wokki20.nl with your request. We will process your deletion request and remove your information from our records.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page, with the last updated date mentioned at the top.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at info@wokki20.nl.

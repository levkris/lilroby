About Us

Welcome to our "About Us" page — the heart of our story and the beginning of something special. You may be wondering: Who are we, and what exactly is this all about?

We are a small but passionate team of friends and creators, united by a shared vision and a common origin: the legacy of Lilroby. Our team includes:

  • wokki20 – A talented web developer who brings the digital infrastructure of our project to life.
  • Max – The guy with the printer (yes, the printer), making your digital creations come to life in the real world.
  • Nathan – The co-founder and the voice behind our online presence. He manages our social media and keeps the community engaged.
  • Silver – The founder and the driving force who helped shape this initiative from the ground up.

Together, we form the core team behind what we lovingly call Lilroby Revive — a tribute project born out of nostalgia, creativity, and the desire to continue a dream that once brought joy to many.

Our Origins: The Legacy of Lilroby

Once upon a time, there was a creator named Lilroby. Through his website and livestreams, he built a small but vibrant community centered around fun, creativity, and even environmental action — such as planting trees. His presence online was inspiring and unique.

But then, one day, Lilroby went offline. No warning. No updates. No way to reach out. It felt like a spark had suddenly vanished from the internet. His streams stopped, his site disappeared, and his playful yet thoughtful idea faded into silence.

We were part of that community. We missed it — the interactivity, the charm, the connection. And that’s why we decided to bring it back, not as a replacement, but as a revival — a continuation in spirit. A way to carry the torch.

What is Lilroby Revive?

Lilroby Revive is our tribute and our reimagining of Lilroby’s original concept. While we can’t replicate every aspect — such as the tree-planting initiative — we’re building something just as fun, interactive, and community-driven.

At the core of Lilroby Revive is a simple but delightful concept:
You can send digital prints, powered by our custom digital currency called Lilcoins. These prints are not just stored digitally — they are actually printed on a real physical printer. That printer lives in the real world, and you can see it in action through our livestreams and video recordings.

It’s like sending a message into the void and seeing it come to life — right in front of you, on paper.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster creativity, connection, and fun — through a blend of technology, art, and nostalgia. We're here to:

  • Provide a playful digital platform that connects people to something physical and tangible.
  • Honor and revive the creative spirit of Lilroby, keeping his legacy alive in a modern and evolving form.
  • Build a welcoming, tight-knit online community where anyone can be a part of the story.

This is more than just a website. It’s a tribute. A community. A shared dream. And you’re invited to be part of it.
